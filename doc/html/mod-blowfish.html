BLOWFISH MODULE     Last modified 24th March 2000

Eggdrop can encrypt your userfile, so users can have secure passwords. Please note, that when you change your encryption method later (i.e. using other modules like a md5 module) you can't use your current userfile anymore.
Attention, eggdrop will not start without a chosen encryption method.
